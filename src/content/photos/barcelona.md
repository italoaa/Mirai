These photos where all taken through the barred window of my room. This room was 6 meters squared actually, I have never had to fit in such a small space. It was super interesting experience, having to be super organised and using the space as efficiently as I could. Although the room was small to be honest this window was amazing. I love windows and this one was almost the size of the wall (mostly because the wall was small).
